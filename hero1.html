ROBERT PEEL

Robert Peel was a British Conservative Statesman and held the position of Prime Minster two times. He was most known for founding the Metropolitan Police Service and being a Founder of the Modern Conservative Party in the Early British Colonial Period which was the development of the British Empire (1788-1850)​
